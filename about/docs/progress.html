Project Progress Updates

Stay up to date with the latest milestones, fieldwork, and achievements from the Digital N-of-1 Trials in Ghana project.

June 2025 – Ethics Clearance Secured

We are pleased to announce that we have secured full ethics clearance from the Ghana Health Service Ethics Review Committee. This important milestone allows us to move forward with all planned project activities, ensuring that our research meets the highest standards of ethical conduct and participant protection.

July 2025 – Stakeholder Engagement Ongoing

Stakeholder engagement activities are currently ongoing. We are meeting with clinicians, traditional medicine experts, and community leaders to introduce the project, gather input, and foster collaboration. These sessions are vital for building trust, ensuring local relevance, and co-designing effective research protocols with all key partners.

More updates coming soon!

Check back regularly for the latest news and progress reports as the project advances.
